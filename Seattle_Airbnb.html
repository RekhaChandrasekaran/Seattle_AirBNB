How to get most value out of your rent out property in Seattle?¶

Understand lodging services in Seattle using Airbnb data.

Business Understanding¶

Airbnb is one of the most popular marketplaces for offering lodging and homestay services. If I’m a homeowner in Seattle, how can I get the most value out of my rent-out property?
I’m interested in answering the following three questions.

  1. What are the most important features that estimate the rental price?
  2. What top amenities should I offer in my rent-out place?
  3. What are the most requested neighborhoods in Seattle?
